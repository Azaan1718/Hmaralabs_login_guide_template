```
Introduction

Welcome to HamaraLabs. This guide helps students log in to the HamaraLabs platform and access their assigned activities. Follow the instructions and screenshots provided below to successfully sign in and begin your learning journey.

This guide is designed for new users and provides a simple, beginner-friendly walkthrough of the login process.

```

HAMARALABS LOGIN GUIDE

STEP 1 :

>>CLICK THE WEBSITE GIVEN HERE : hamaralabs

>>YOU WILL SEE THE LOGIN PORTAL AS SHOWN BELOW

>>ENTER THE USERNAME AND PASSWORD TO LOGIN

>>THE HOME PAGE AFTER LOGGING IN WILL APPEAR LIKE THE IMAGE BELOW

STEP 2 :

>>AFTER LOGGIN IN CLICK THE OPTION "STUDENT TINKERING ACTIVITIES" TO VIEW THE ACTIVITIES ASSIGNED TO YOU

>>AFTER SELECTING STUDENT TINKERING ACTIVITIES YOU CAN OBSERVE THE ACTIVITIES ON THE PAGE AS SHOWN BELOW

STEP 3 :

>>AFTER COMPLETING AN ACTIVITY CLICK ON THE PENCIL TOOLBAR TO UPLOAD YOUR WORK

>>THE PAGE OPENS AS SHOWN BELOW

>>UPLOAD YOUR WORK AND WAIT UNTIL IT GETS UPLOADED

STEP 3 :

>>AFTER UPLAODING YOUR WORK CLICK ON THE MODIFY STATUS OPTION BESIDE PENCIL TOOLBAR TO UPDATE THE STATUS OF THR ACTIVITY

>>YOU CAN SEE MANY OPTIONS AFTER CLICKING "MODIFY STATUS" OPTION AS SHOWN BELOW

Thank You

You have successfully learned how to access the HamaraLabs platform. Continue exploring the portal and complete your assigned activities.
